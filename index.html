About Me

Engineer turned web developer. Graduated from the University of California, Irvine with a Bachelor degree in Mechanical Engineering and a minor in Spanish. As an engineering major I was required to take one programming course, which at the time was Fortran, and really enjoyed the logical aspect of coding.

After graduating and working for some time, I decided to make a switch in careers and took some computer science courses which included intro to different languages (such as C++, Java, Python and ASP.NET). I enrolled in a full-stack web development program, and now I am currently pursuing a career in the field.
